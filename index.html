About

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the ETH Zurich, where I am advised by Prof. Dr. Massimo Filippini.

My research focuses on applying microeconomic theory insights to the real world data and using econometric methods to test the predictions of the theory.

Initially, I also worked on axiomatic choice theory during my Master's (of Philosophy) thesis.
